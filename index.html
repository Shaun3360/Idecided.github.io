About I DECIDED

Our Upskill Course is designed specifically for recent high school graduates—cutting through the noise with actionable steps and real-world insights. We help you navigate the challenges of life after matric by providing practical lessons, expert mentorship, and a supportive community.

"I DECIDED" isn’t just a course—it’s a movement. We’re here to teach you not only what to do but how to build your own success. In addition to the core course content, our program kickstarts community engagement initiatives. Volunteers and organizations join us to support workshops and real-world skill-building that make a tangible impact.

Our Vision

To empower young graduates with the skills, mindset, and confidence needed to navigate life after school and achieve their dreams.
